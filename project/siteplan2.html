Latter-day Saint Book Club - Site Plan

Site Name

Domain Name: LDS Book Club

Reason: The name was selected to represent the focus on creating a platform for Latter-day Saint book clubs, offering resources and a community space for readers.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide a comprehensive platform dedicated to Latter-day Saint book clubs. It will feature resources such as discussion guides, book quizzes, games, food and drink suggestions, and playlist recommendations. It will also host editorial content like author interviews, opinion essays, book reviews, reading challenges, and giveaways, to encourage the growth and engagement of book clubs.

Scenarios

1. What are some recommended books for spiritual growth?

2. How can I join a book discussion or find book club meetings in my area?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Bright Blue (#00A3FF) – Used for the site header, buttons, and major highlights.

Secondary Color: Dark Blue (#1E2B3A) – Used for backgrounds and footers.

Typography

Heading Font: Agdasima, used for all main headings.

Body Font: Roboto Slab, used for general text.
